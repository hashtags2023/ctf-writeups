## Task 2 — Security Analyst

Security analysts (blue team) monitor networks, investigate alerts,
and respond to incidents. They piece together potential security events
to decide if further action is needed.

> Security analysts play a significant role in an organisation's _____?

**Answer:** `defence`

---

## Task 3 — Security Engineer

Security engineers build and maintain the systems that protect an
organisation — firewalls, IDS, etc. They're the architects of
cyber security.

> What device would a Security Engineer be responsible for maintaining?

**Answer:** `Intrusion Detection System`

---

## Task 4 — Penetration Tester

Penetration testers (ethical hackers) safely break into systems
under a formal agreement to find vulnerabilities before real
attackers do.

> What process do penetration testers follow when testing an organisation?

**Answer:** `engagement`

## Key Takeaways

1. Blue team (analyst/engineer) suits detail-oriented, methodical thinkers.
2. Red team/pentesting suits people who enjoy breaking things legally.
3. 3.5M+ unfilled roles globally — demand is massive across all paths.
4. No specific background needed; curiosity and problem-solving matter most.
